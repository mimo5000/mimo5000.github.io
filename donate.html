Donate to the Morrison Preperatory School

Donations are currently processed in partnership with the University of Toronto's International Centre for Disability and Rehabilitation, and as such it is a two step process. Please complete our donation form which will redirect you to the ICDR donation page, where you will enter the amount of your donation in the box for ICDR-Kenya. Proceed to your cart and in the additional information box, enter "Morrison School".
You may request a card to use in the event the donation was made on behalf of someone else as a gift.

$25 - School Supplies

Your donation of $25 will buy books and supplies for three students.

$50 - Teachers' Salaries

Your donation of $50 will supplement three teacher's salaries for three months

$100 - Micro Loan

Your donation of $100 will be used as a revolving loan for parents in the community who have income generating ideas but lack the start up funds required to make their small business a reality.

Other Amount

Your generous donation of any amount will be used for ongoing projects at the school.
