Morrison Preparatory School Donations

Donations are currently processed in partnership with the University of Toronto's
International Centre for Disability and Rehabilitation, and as such it is a two-step process.
Please complete our donation form which will redirect you to the ICDR donation page,
where you will enter the amount of your donation in the box for ICDR-Kenya.
Proceed to your cart and in the additional information box, enter "Morrison School".
You may request a card to use in the event the donation is on behalf of someone else as a gift.

$25 - School Supplies

Your donation of $25 will buy books and supplies for three students.

$50 - Teachers' Salaries

Your donation of $50 will supplement three teacher's salaries for three months.

$100 - Micro Loan

Your donation will be used as a revolving loan for parents in the community who have income generating ideas but lack the start up funds required to make their small business a reality.

Other Amount

Your generous donation of any amount will be used for ongoing projects at the school, such as printers and supplies, new classrooms, safety devices, and more.
