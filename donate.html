Donate to the Morrison Preperatory School


Donations are currently processed in partnership with the University of Toronto's International Centre for Disability and Rehabilitation, and as such it is a two step process. Please click on "contribute," which will take you to the ICDR donation page, and enter the amount of your donation in the box for "ICDR-Kenya." Proceed to your "cart" and in the "additional information" box, enter "Morrison School." Next, please fill out the donor information form on this page, including your name and email address. In the comments section, please include the amount of your donation. If requested, you will receive a card to use in the event that the donation was made on behalf of someone else as a gift.

$25 - School Supplies

Your donation of $25 will buy books and supplies for three students.

$50 - Teachers' Salaries

Your donation of $50 will supplement three teacher's salaries for three months

$100 - Micro Loan

Your donation of $100 will be used as a revolving loan for parents in the community who have income generating ideas but lack the start up funds required to make their small business a reality.

Other Amount

Your generous donation of any amount will be used for ongoing projects at the school.
